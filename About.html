About Us

Founded in 2018 by local volunteers, Ubuntu Community Food Bank is dedicated to reducing hunger and promoting nutrition awareness across Johannesburg.

Our Mission

To fight hunger by ensuring that vulnerable families have access to healthy food.

Our Vision

A future where no South African family goes to bed hungry.
