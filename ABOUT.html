About Us

Founded in 2018 by local volunteers, Ubuntu Community Food Bank is dedicated to reducing hunger and promoting nutrition awareness across Johannesburg. Our name "Ubuntu" reflects our philosophy: "I am because we are" - we believe in community support and collective responsibility.

Our Mission

To fight hunger by ensuring that vulnerable families have access to healthy, nutritious food while promoting sustainable solutions to food insecurity through education and community empowerment.

Our Vision

A future where no South African family goes to bed hungry, and every community has the resources and knowledge to maintain food security and nutritional well-being.

Our Journey

2018

Founded by community volunteers with a small food distribution center in Johannesburg Central, serving 50 families monthly

2020

Expanded to Soweto branch and launched School Nutrition Program, reaching 500+ children weekly

2022

Opened Alexandra branch and introduced Nutrition Awareness Workshops, training 200+ families

2024

Reached milestone of serving 5,000+ families across Johannesburg with comprehensive food support
