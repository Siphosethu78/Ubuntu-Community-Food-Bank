Our Programs

Discover how we're fighting hunger and promoting nutrition in Johannesburg through our comprehensive programs.

Monthly Program

Food Parcel Drives

Monthly distribution of nutritious food parcels to vulnerable families across our three branches in Johannesburg.

  • Monthly distributions at all locations
  • Nutritionally balanced food parcels
  • Family-sized portions for 2 weeks
  • Special dietary accommodations available
1,200+ families served monthly
School Program

School Initiatives

Partnering with local schools to provide daily meals and nutrition education to children in need.

  • Daily nutritious school meals
  • Nutrition and health education
  • Weekend food packs for families
  • Teacher training programs
15 schools partnered across Johannesburg
Education Program

Nutrition Awareness

Workshops and training sessions teaching families about healthy eating, budgeting, and meal planning.

  • Weekly nutrition workshops
  • Cooking demonstrations
  • Budget-friendly meal planning
  • Community garden training
200+ families trained annually
